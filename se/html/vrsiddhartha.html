A Place to Glow

Velagapudi Ramakrishna Siddhartha Engineering College, a self financing institute established in the year 1977.The institution is ideally located in a vast expanse of 40 acres of land on the out skirts of Vijayawada city at a distance of about 6 Kms from the city centre. The departmental buildings are located in close proximity of each other with wide open spaces all around.
